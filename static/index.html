Synopsis

One Piece is the story of a boy named Monkey D. Luffy, who was inspired by Shanks, a pirate who saved his life, to become the King of the Pirates. At the beginning of the series, twenty-four years before the current story, a pirate named Gol D. Roger discovered the legendary One Piece treasure, and declared that anyone was free to take it. This sparked a new age of piracy, and Luffy set out to find the One Piece and become the new King of the Pirates.
